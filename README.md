## Business Insights

### 1. Regional Performance
- North region generated the highest revenue (₹239,500).
- Indicates strong demand concentration in the North zone.
- **Business Action:** Increase inventory allocation and regional marketing spend in North.

### 2. Product Performance
- Laptops contributed the highest revenue (₹338,000) despite fewer orders.
- Mobiles have high volume but lower revenue per unit.
- **Business Action:**  
  - Promote premium laptop models  
  - Introduce upselling strategies for mobile accessories

### 3. Revenue Mix Observation
- Revenue is unevenly distributed across regions and products.
- Dependency on a single high-performing region can increase risk.
- **Business Action:**  
  - Strengthen underperforming regions (East, West)  
  - Run targeted promotions to balance revenue streams
